Our Products & Services

Animal Feed

  • Cow Feed: Organic and nutrient-rich feed for healthy cows, designed to improve milk production and weight gain.
  • Pig Feed: Balanced feed formulated to promote fast growth and strong immune systems for pigs.
  • Goat & Sheep Feed: Specially mixed feed to support wool growth, fertility, and overall health.

Livestock Services

  • Artificial Insemination: We provide professional insemination using high-quality male genetics to improve herd productivity.
  • Animal Crossbreeding: Helping farmers develop stronger, healthier, and more productive livestock through careful crossbreeding.
  • Veterinary Services: Our animal doctor provides regular health checkups, medication, and emergency care.

Meat Supply

  • Premium Meat: Supply of high-quality meat to restaurants and butcheries, available in different grades for all customer needs.

Wool & Mohair

  • Wool: Clean and high-quality wool from sheep for textile and craft purposes.
  • Mohair: Soft mohair from goats, ideal for clothing and accessories.
